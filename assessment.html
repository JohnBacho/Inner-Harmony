Recovering From Mental Health Issues

Women are twice as likely as men to experience mood disorders like depression, OCD, anxiety, bipolar, and more. Mood disorders are complex conditions and require treatment. Roughly 40% of women report that their mental health has been severely affected by their menstrual cycle, pregnancy, post-partum, or menopause. At Inner Harmony, we take pride in assisting women who are struggling with mental health issues. We work with several specialists who can assist women of all ages and backgrounds. We have specialists who work with women who are going through their menstrual cycle, menopause, or even women who are going through postpartum depression. Inner Harmony offers resources and supportive systems to help those struggling to receive the help they need. Through our resources, we can help women of all ages manage their mental health struggles easier.

While you, or someone you know struggle with mental health issues, you are not alone. We will assist you in finding a support system to assure you receive the support you need.

If you, or someone you know are struggling, please contact us for a complimentary and confidential consultation.

Please fill out the questionnaire so we can better assist you. The more information provided to us, the more likely we will be able to assure you support and resources.

After you submit the form on this page, you will be assigned a licensed specialist who will schedule a confidential, free appointment. Specialists are assigned based on your answers to the questionnaire, and your specialist will specialize in issues that either pertain to your situation or are similar to your situation. They will meet with you and inform you of the various resources that may benefit you and gain an understanding of your situation. Our teams work closely with you to ensure that we foster a supportive, understanding environment for you.
